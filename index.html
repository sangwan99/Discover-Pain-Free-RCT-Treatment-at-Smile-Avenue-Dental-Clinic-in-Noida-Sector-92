How RCT Treatment is Done at Smile Avenue Dental Clinic

RCT treatment is an intricate process but RCT treatment in Noida Sector 92 offers the most efficient solution. Root Canal Treatment (RCT) at Smile Avenue Dental Clinic is a meticulous process designed to alleviate pain and preserve your natural tooth. Our expert dentists in Noida Sector 92 follow a step-by-step approach to ensure the success and comfort of your treatment.

1. Initial Consultation and Examination

Your journey begins with a thorough examination by one of our skilled dentists. They will assess the condition of your tooth using advanced diagnostic tools such as digital X-rays. This helps in determining the extent of infection or damage that necessitates RCT.

2. Local Anesthesia

Once the diagnosis is confirmed, local anesthesia is administered to numb the affected area. This ensures that you remain comfortable throughout the procedure and experience minimal discomfort.

3. Accessing the Tooth

After numbing the area, our dentist creates a small access hole in the tooth to reach the infected pulp chamber. This step allows for the removal of infected tissue and bacteria.

4. Cleaning and Shaping the Root Canals

Using specialized instruments, our dentists carefully clean and shape the root canals to remove all traces of infection. This meticulous process ensures that the canals are prepared to receive a filling material.

5. Filling the Canals

Once cleaned, the root canals are filled with a biocompatible material called gutta-percha. This material seals the canals to prevent re-infection and supports the structure of the tooth.

6. Restoration of the Tooth

After completing the root canal treatment, our dentist will recommend restoring the tooth with a crown or filling, depending on the extent of damage. This step aims to strengthen the tooth and restore its functionality.

7. Follow-Up Care

At Smile Avenue Dental Clinic, your care doesn't end with the procedure. We provide comprehensive post-operative instructions to ensure proper healing and maintenance of your treated tooth. Regular follow-up appointments allow us to monitor your progress and address any concerns.
